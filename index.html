About

I've been interested in programming ever since a game called Destiny came out in 2014. There was an application that came out shortly after the game came out called ishtar commander. This let you access your character on your phone or on the browser and transfer gear freely without having to go to the main hub where your gear vault is.

This was so interesting to me, I had to know how the hell this was happening. After going through a coding tutorial hell, I gave up. Here and there i'd look at some post on /r/programminghumor and i'd wish I was able to understand. Sooner or later I convinced my best friend to go to a coding bootcamp with me. We chose General Assembly Chicago. Our jouney's started September 2018.

Leash Pals

(Ruby/Sinatra/ReactJS/Postgres) This app is a Full Stack Web App that is currently being built and is used to schedule dog walks with registered Walkers. There is an interface for walkers to setup their available schedule and approve scheduled walks, and an interface for pet owners to register their dogs and schedule walks with registered Walkers.. This application implements a user authentication and permission system, as well as calendar scheduling. It is still being developed, don't hate on the CSS :p

InkBerrie

This was made with Express and node.
